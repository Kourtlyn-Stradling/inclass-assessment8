Chickpea Salad

The Perfect Spring or Summer Recipe

Author: Natasha of Natashaskitchen.com

Servings: 6 as a side salad

Calories: 302

Prep Time: 30 mins

Recommended Cool Time: At least 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 Tbsp lemon joice, from 1 large lemon
  • 1 garlic clove, pressed or minced
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt, or to taste
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper, or to taste
  • Lemon pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 English cucumber, halved and sliced
  • 15 oz chickpeas, or garbanzo beans, drained, rinsed
  • 1/2 medium red onion, thinly diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup cilatro, chopped
  • 4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 1/2 chicken breasts diced, cooked

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken breast into cubes no bigger than 1 inch by 1 inch. Spray or lightly oil a pan to cook the chicken in. Once it's on the stove, put lemon pepper, salt, and pepper to taste. Cook on a medium to low heat. (15 mins )
  2. Combine the dressing ingredients in a small bowl: 3 tbsp olive oil, 3 tbsp lemon juice, 1 pressed garlic clove, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/8 tsp pepper. Whisk to combine (or shake together in a small mason jar).
  3. Combine the remaining chickpea salad ingredients in a salad bowl.
  4. Add the dressing to taste (we use all of it) and toss gently to coat.
